Our Story

Built for the Stars

The Mission

Patineto Anacleto was born from a simple frustration: cities designed for cars, not people. Our founders — engineers, designers, and lifelong urban dwellers — set out to reimagine how we move through the spaces we call home.

We believe that sustainable mobility shouldn't be a compromise. It should be thrilling, beautiful, and built to last. Every scooter we make is a declaration that the future of transport is electric, smart, and unapologetically cool.

🚀
Fearless Innovation

We push technical limits on every model. If it can be faster, smarter, or lighter — we make it so.

🌍
Planet First

Zero emissions, recyclable packaging, carbon-neutral factories. Sustainability isn't a feature, it's our foundation.

🏙️
Urban by DNA

Designed on city streets, tested in real traffic. We ride what we build, in the cities you live in.

The Name

Who is Anacleto? He's the astronaut who traded his rocket for a scooter — because he realized the most interesting frontier isn't outer space. It's the city block. He's curious, bold, and always moving forward.

Anacleto is our mascot, our philosophy, and our promise: explore further, travel lighter, arrive with style.

The Technology

Every Patineto Anacleto is engineered with aerospace-grade aluminum frames, high-density lithium cells sourced from certified ethical suppliers, and BLDC hub motors tuned for urban terrain. Our proprietary EcoRegen braking recovers up to 15% of energy on every ride.

Our companion app — available on iOS and Android — provides real-time telemetry, predictive maintenance alerts, custom riding modes, and over-the-air firmware updates. Your scooter gets better over time, not worse.

The Community

Over 120,000 riders across 28 countries call themselves Patineto Anacleto riders. From Valencia to Vancouver, from Berlin to Bangkok — we're a global crew united by the love of clean, fast, street-level exploration.
